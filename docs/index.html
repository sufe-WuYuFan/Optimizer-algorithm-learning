Research Interests

My current interests connect optimization algorithms with the linear-algebraic structures that govern curvature, conditioning, and iteration dynamics.

A

Mathematical Optimization

Gradient methods, mirror descent, adaptive algorithms, convergence analysis, and the geometry of preconditioning.

B

Matrix Optimization

Adam, Shampoo, SOAP, Muon, PolarGrad, matrix functions, polar decomposition, and Kronecker structure.

C

Numerical Linear Algebra

QR, SVD, Cholesky, positive definiteness, spectral analysis, conditioning, and stable computation.
